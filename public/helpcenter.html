Troubleshooting Camera Issues

Not seeing your camera in Camera Studio? Here are quick fixes for the most common problems

Camera Access Not Allowed

Your browser needs permission to use the camera.

  • Click “Allow Camera” in the popup
  • Select Allow while visiting the site

Camera Blocked in Settings

You may have denied access earlier.

  • Click the icon next to the address bar
  • Find Camera and toggle access on
  • Refresh the page to apply changes

Camera Already in Use

Another app (like Zoom or Meet) is using
your camera.

  • Close other apps
  • Refresh Camera Studio

Switch to Another Camera

One camera is busy, but another is available

  • Use the dropdown in Camera Studio to select a
    different camera.

Can’t Change Resolution

If another app is using your camera, resolution/ratio
settings will be locked.

  • Close other apps
  • Click Refresh

Need More Help?

Here are additional FAQs to help you get the most out of Camera Studio

It’s a camera control and presentation app.

This is a web-based Progressive Web App (PWA) that runs in the Chrome browser. It lets you view, control, and manipulate the live feed from a document camera, making it ideal for classrooms, remote learning, product demos, tutorials, and virtual presentations.

No

This app is not a replacement for Google Meet, Zoom or Microsoft Teams. It’s designed to run alongside these platforms, allowing you to share your document camera view during a call.

ChromeOS devices and UVC-compliant document cameras

This app is designed for ChromeOS and runs directly in the Chrome browser. It supports all UVC-compliant document cameras, meaning plug-and-play compatibility with most modern USB cameras.

Dedicated controls for a better presentation

Key Features

Streamlined Camera Setup

Pick your camera. Set your resolution. Done

Switch between connected UVC cameras, select resolution, or adjust aspect ratio — all from a simple and clean interface

Camera Controls

Focus. Zoom. Exposure

Easily adjust focus, zoom, and exposure to fine-tune your feed. Whether you’re zooming in on text or adjusting for lighting, the app gives you all the control you need to showcase your content clearly

Mirror Over X & Y Axis

Flip the Perspective

Easily switch perspectives by flipping your video feed horizontally or vertically. Perfect for aligning content exactly how you want it displayed.

Seamless Rotation

Snap to 90° turns

Rotate your live feed in 90° increments to adjust for portrait or landscape views. Quickly switch orientations to present documents, images, or videos without hassle

Freeze Frame

Pause and focus

Freeze your live feed with a single click to lock in the moment. Perfect for pausing video demonstrations or focusing on specific content while annotating or explaining

On-Screen Annotation

Draw. Type. Highlight

Mark up your video feed in real-time. Use drawing tools, text labels, and colors to enhance your lesson, walkthrough, or explanation on the spot

Capture & Store with Ease

Local or Cloud

Capture images and save them directly to your device or Google Drive. All your content is stored safely, with easy access for future reference or sharing
